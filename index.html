About the Chief Medical Officer

Mandi, Himachal Pradesh

The Chief Medical Officer (CMO) of Mandi district plays a pivotal role in managing and overseeing the public health infrastructure of the region. The office is responsible for planning, implementing, and monitoring healthcare services, including hospitals, primary health centres, immunization programs, and disease control initiatives.

With a commitment to improving community health and ensuring equitable access to medical services, the CMO’s office coordinates with various departments, NGOs, and local bodies to respond to public health needs, manage emergencies, and promote awareness campaigns across rural and urban areas.

The CMO also leads the district's efforts in national health programs such as the National Health Mission (NHM), tuberculosis control, maternal and child health, and more — striving for a healthier Mandi.

N.K. Bhardwaj
