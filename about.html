Welcome to Health & Family Welfare Department, Mandi

Himachal Pradesh, particularly Mandi District, offers various health and family welfare initiatives aimed at improving public health and medical services.

Key Services and Programs

  • Primary Health Centers (PHCs): First contact points in rural areas, offering maternal and child care, immunizations, and general treatments.
  • Community Health Centers (CHCs): Provide specialized services including emergency care and advanced diagnostics.
  • District Hospital: Offers specialized care in cardiology, orthopedics, gynecology, and more.
  • National Health Mission (NHM): Initiatives like Janani Suraksha Yojana (JSY) ensure accessible and affordable healthcare.
  • Immunization Programs: Regular drives protect children and pregnant women from preventable diseases.
  • Family Welfare Services: Reproductive health and family planning programs.
  • Health Awareness Campaigns: Programs promoting sanitation, hygiene, and disease prevention.
  • Emergency Services: Ambulances and medical response units for rapid intervention.

Contact Information

For queries or services, please contact your nearest health center or the district health office:

  • District Health Office: Get details on available health services and facilities in Mandi.
  • Local PHCs/CHCs: Contact details are posted locally at each center for specific services.

For official updates and accurate contact information, visit the Himachal Pradesh Health & Family Welfare Department website or get in touch with your local government health office.
